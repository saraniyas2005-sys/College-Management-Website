Admissions Open for 2025 at St. Anne's First Grade College

How to Apply

We're excited to have you join the St. Anne's First Grade College community. Follow the steps below to complete your application:

  1. Step 1: Fill out the online application form.
  2. Step 2: Upload your necessary documents (ID proof, educational certificates, etc.).
  3. Step 3: Pay the application fee online through our secure payment portal.
  4. Step 4: Submit your application and await further instructions for interviews or entrance exams (if applicable).

Documents Required

  • Recent passport-size photograph
  • Copy of your last academic mark sheet
  • Government-issued ID (Aadhar card, passport, etc.)
  • Transfer certificate (if applicable)
  • Caste certificate (for reserved category students)
