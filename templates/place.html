Placement & Career Development

The Placement Cell at St. Anne’s FGC empowers students with holistic career readiness. We offer:

  • Career Guidance & Work Shops: Personality development, resume building, aptitude tests, mock interviews, and group discussions.
  • Certified Training: Industry-aligned certifications in areas like Digital Marketing, Data Analysis, Soft Skills, etc.
  • Internships: Partnerships with reputed organizations for hands-on professional exposure.
  • Campus Drives: Recruitments by MNCs, public sector firms, startups—frequent on-campus job fairs.
  • Alumni Connect: Strong alumni network offering mentorship, internships, and placement referrals.
  • 24×7 Support: One-on-one guidance, resume reviews, and mock interview sessions by industry experts and in-house trainers.

Our graduates have secured placements in top companies like TCS, Wipro, Infosys, Deloitte, Cognizant, PwC, and emerging startups—excelling in roles such as Business Analyst, Software Developer, HR Executive, Digital Marketer, and Research Associate.
