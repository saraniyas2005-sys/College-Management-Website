Principal’s Desk

Education is the most powerful tool for transformation. At St. Anne’s First Grade College for Women, Miller Road, our aim is to ignite minds, instill values, and nurture women of strength, intellect, and purpose. Rooted in our motto “Love and Service”, we help our students evolve not just as achievers but as compassionate citizens.

Inspired by our visionary founder Bishop Stephen Louis Charbonnaux and guided by the values of Jesus Christ, we empower women with education that reflects academic excellence and spiritual wisdom. Our college is a sanctuary where students explore their potential and prepare for a life of contribution and leadership.

Every Annite is mentored to grow intellectually and emotionally through personalized attention, innovative teaching methods, and a nurturing environment. Our focus lies not just on academic performance but on holistic development—shaping responsible individuals ready to make meaningful changes in society.

I welcome each aspiring young woman to embark on a transformative journey with us—where learning meets purpose, and knowledge becomes a light that guides.

Dr. Sr. Aneecia
Principal
M.Sc, Ph.D (Psychology)
